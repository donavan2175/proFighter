Who Are We

We are a group of fighters from different backgrounds, who all have one thing in common. That is we love the sport of pro fighting. We are a team who trains hard, and push each other to improve. Some of us are professionals, and some are brand new. We guarentee that as long as you do what's asked, and work hard. You will see results.

What To Expect

upon arival, you will be greeted by the owner who will give you a tour of the facility. He will explain how everything works, and answer all of your questions. After the tour he will have a one on one with you to discuss your goals, why are you there, and what exactly you're looking for. Once that is done, you will have a start date. On your start date you will meet your teammates and coaches. Gear will be provided.
